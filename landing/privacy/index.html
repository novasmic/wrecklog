Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how NovaSmic Pty Ltd ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and stores information when you use the WreckLog mobile application ("the App").

1. Information We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (if you create an account)
  • Authentication details via Firebase Authentication

App Data

  • Vehicles, parts, listings, pricing, and related data you enter into the App

This data may be stored locally on your device and, if you choose to sign in, securely synced to cloud servers.

2. How Your Data Is Stored

WreckLog uses a combination of:

  • Local storage (on your device)
  • Cloud storage via Firebase (Google Cloud services)

When signed in, your data is securely stored in Firestore and associated with your user account.

We implement access controls to ensure that users can only access their own data.

3. Analytics

WreckLog uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data, including:

  • App interactions (e.g. parts added, vehicles created)
  • Feature usage
  • General app performance data

This information is used to improve the App and does not identify you personally.

4. In-App Purchases

WreckLog offers optional in-app purchases (subscriptions) through:

  • Google Play Billing
  • Apple App Store (where applicable)

All payment processing is handled by Google or Apple. We do not receive or store your payment details.

For more information:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

5. Data Control

You have full control over your data:

  • You may use the App without creating an account
  • Signing in enables cloud backup and sync
  • You may delete your data from the App at any time

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect your data using secure cloud infrastructure provided by Google Firebase.

However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

7. Ads

WreckLog does not display third-party advertisements.

8. Children's Privacy

WreckLog is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last updated" date above.
